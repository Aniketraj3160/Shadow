About S.H.A.D.O.W

Crimes have been increasing in India and crime rates have hit an all time high. What the people see, they don't report due to a variety of reasons including but not limited to violence, death threats, and even prosecution by the very authorities that they are supposed to complain to. This is a humble attempt to remedy the aforementioned problems by providing a safe anonymous bubble within which the people can report crimes and let the authorities know what is happening outside their vision.

How It Works

S.H.A.D.O.W is a website that tries to use the goodwill of the masses to provide useful and up-to-date information to the authorities without the risk of identity leaks.

The Mobile App

The mobile application of S.H.A.D.O.W can be downloaded and used as a substitute for the original app. Using the mobile application does not in any way reveal your identity to anyone, including us. It might even be better.
